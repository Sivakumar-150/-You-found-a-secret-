### 🌱 What I Do
- Conduct **GHG inventories** & **carbon footprint assessments** using **BRSR**, **GRI**, **TCFD**, **SBTi**, and other leading frameworks
- Develop & analyze key **environmental KPIs** — energy intensity, water efficiency, waste diversion, Scope 1-2-3 emissions
